Who We Are

Nigeria's Premier
Luxury Real Estate Firm

RokHaven Realty was established in 2019 with a clear mandate: to elevate the standard of luxury real estate service in Nigeria. We exist not as a listing directory, but as a professional advisory firm — one that curates, verifies, and presents properties of exceptional calibre to clients who demand nothing less.

From our base in Lagos, we serve high-net-worth individuals, corporate executives, diaspora investors, and discerning buyers across Nigeria and internationally. Every engagement is handled with the discretion and professionalism befitting the scale of the transactions we facilitate.

The name RokHaven reflects our founding philosophy. "Rok" represents solidity — the enduring strength of real property as a wealth-building asset. "Haven" represents sanctuary — the irreplaceable feeling of a home that is truly yours.
